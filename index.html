Discovery Cave

Disovery Cave is an educational game for preschool children. Created by veteran educator, Vivian Aggrey, it is a fun way for kids to learn important skills across multiple subject matters like reading, math, science and art. The cave based theme allows free exploration, and encourages children to learn at their own pace and choose their own experience. Perfect for at home supplementary content or transitional moments in the classroom, Discovery Cave is a growing world of learning and positive challenges.
